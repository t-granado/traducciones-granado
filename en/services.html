SERVICE OFFERING

We work with specialization in the following areas:

Translation

EN-ES, EN-VAL

Our guiding principle is to provide a service tailored to each client’s needs. Depending on the characteristics of the project, we will propose either human translation or machine translation combined with a more or less intensive post-editing process. Our goal is to optimise resources while always guaranteeing the highest quality outcome.

  • General

    • Human translation of texts of various types, including journalistic and informative content.

  • Legal

    • Human translation of legal texts. Non-certified translations of documents, contracts, certificates, judgments, court orders and other legal materials.

  • Machine translation and post-editing

    • Machine translation carried out using specialised software, always combined with a post-editing process to ensure the quality of the final text. Post-editing may be light or full, depending on the requirements of the project. Proper handling of personal data is fully guaranteed in all machine translation tools we use.

Copy editing

Copy editing ES, VAL

Copy editing and proofreading in Spanish and Valencian. If you need to review a text, assess its linguistic quality or prepare it for publication, our holistic copy editing services cover all types and stages of the revision process.

Interpreting

EN-ES, EN-VAL, ES-EN, VAL-EN

  • Consecutive interpreting

    • Based on notes taken during the speaker’s intervention, the message is translated orally into the target language. The interpretation alternates after each intervention. In these situations, interpreting is carried out in both directions, from language A to language B and from language B to language A. Recommended for meetings, interviews, hearings and negotiations.

  • Intercultural mediation and accompaniment

    • Direct and indirect interpreting in less formal contexts or where conversational flow is not strictly structured. Recommended for procedures in public institutions, in-person applications or informal meetings.
