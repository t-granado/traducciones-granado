RESOURCES FOR TRANSLATORS

Here you will find a range of tools and software that are extremely useful for translation work.

Dictionaries

    Dictionaries are a translator's best friend. It is the most essential resource, enabling us to fully understand every word in the source text. There are multiple types of dictionaries and they can be classified according to different aspects (language combination, subject matter, regional variation...) Below is a selection of key free online dictionaries:

  1. Monolingual (Spanish, English, Valencian)

    1. Diccionario de la lengua española (dictionary of the Spanish language): Real Academia Española's dictionary. https://dle.rae.es/
    2. Diccionario de americanismos (dictionary of americanisms): Asociación de Academias de la Lengua Española. It includes words exclusively from Spanish-speaking countries in America. https://www.asale.org/damer/
    3. Cambridge dictionary: https://dictionary.cambridge.org/
    4. Collins dictionary: https://www.collinsdictionary.com/
    5. Diccionari normatiu valencià (Valencian normative dictionary): https://www.avl.gva.es/
    6. Diccionari de la llengua valenciana (dictionary of the Valencian language): diccionari.llenguavalenciana.com

  2. Bilingual

    1. Wordreference: includes different translation possibilities with examples and usage markers https://www.wordreference.com/es/
    2. Linguee: presents a wide selection of corpora that allow the observation of the term's use in real contexts https://www.linguee.es/
    3. Reverso: offers a multitude of resources (translator, dictionary, context) https://context.reverso.net/traduccion/

  3. Specialized

    1. Law dicitonary: https://dictionary.law.com/
    2. Diccionario panhispánico del español jurídico (Pan-Hispanic dictionary of legal Spanish): https://dpej.rae.es/

Terminology Portals

  1. TERMCAT: compiles a multitude of resources and services, from dictionaries, glossaries, and thematic portals, to content on current Catalan linguistic and terminological affairs.
    2. https://www.termcat.cat/ca

Linguistic enquiries

When drafting the target text (of a translation) or in the copy editing process, it is very useful to have access to portals where doubts can be quickly resolved and academic recommendations are explained.

  1. Fundeu (advised by the RAE): https://www.fundeu.es/
  2. Optimot (offered by the Departament de Política Lingüística, with the collaboration of the Institut d'Estudis Catalans and TERMCAT): https://aplicacions.llengua.gencat.cat

Machine Translation

Although the effectiveness of machine translation has advanced considerably in recent years, some higher-quality programs can be distinguished:

  1. DeepL (there is an open version and a paid version) https://www.deepl.com/es/translator
  2. ETranslation (exclusive to European Union members, requires registration) https://translation.ec.europa.eu/
  3. Reverso: https://www.reverso.net/traducci%C3%B3n-texto
