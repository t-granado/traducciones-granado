PROFESSIONAL ETHICS

  • Privacy and data protection

    • All texts are deleted one month after the completion of the translation project. Client data are stored solely for internal purposes and are never shared with third parties. Any personal or sensitive information contained in the texts or provided by the client is automatically deleted once the project is completed.

  • Adaptability

    • We adapt our services and resources to the specific needs of each assignment and client, with the aim of delivering high-quality results in the most efficient way.

  • Quality and guarantee

    • We devote all our knowledge, skills and resources to each project to ensure that the final result meets the client’s expectations. All translations undergo several stages of revision and proofreading. If the client is not satisfied, an external evaluation will be carried out and, if the agreed requirements are not met, a revised translation will be provided incorporating all requested changes.

  • Fairness and trust

    • We establish an equal and respectful relationship with our clients, since we believe that the interests and needs of both parties must be respected. Moreover, we foster communication with the client when dealing with translation challenges or stylistic decisions.

  • Inclusion and accessibility

    • All our services can be adapted for people with disabilities or physical limitations. Also, we are committed to inclusive language from a decolonial and gender perspective. Contact us to learn more about accessibility options.
