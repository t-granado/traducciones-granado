Welcome!

My name is Alicia Granado and I am a professional translator. I hold a degree in Translation and Intercultural Mediation from the University of Valencia and a Master’s degree in Institutional Translation from the University of Alicante. My working languages are Spanish, Valencian (Catalan) and English, with the language combinations EN–ES and EN–VAL. I am specialised in legal translation, machine translation and post-editing, proofreading, and public service interpreting.

On this website you will find information about the linguistic and translation services we offer, as well as the ethical principles that guide our professional practice. In addition, a selection of useful translation resources is provided for professionals or anyone interested in this field.
